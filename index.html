About RouteWeaver

Built for the Stranded Traveler

Every peak season, millions of Indians find themselves stuck with waitlisted tickets and no viable alternatives. Power users spend hours manually checking transit hubs. RouteWeaver automates all of that.

Our graph-traversal engine (inspired by Dijkstra's algorithm) maps the Indian rail network as nodes and edges, then discovers optimized multi-leg paths in seconds.

🛡️
The 2-Hour Safety Buffer

Every connection in a Smart Route has at least 2 hours of layover — enough margin for typical Indian Railways delays. No more missed connections.

⚠️ MVP Disclaimer: RouteWeaver uses a simulated network for demonstration. Live IRCTC booking and real-time tracking are out of scope for this version.
